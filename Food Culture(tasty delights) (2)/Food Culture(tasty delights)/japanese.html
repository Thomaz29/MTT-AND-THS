Traditional Dishes from Japan

  • Sushi. A popular dish in Japan, sushi is also well loved in the Western world.

  • Tempura. Tempura is commonly made by deep-frying seafood, vegetables, or prawns.

  • Sashimi. A Japanese dish of thinly-sliced raw food, usually fish and seafood.

  • Miso Soup. A traditional food that has supported the dietary life of the Japanese people.

  • Soba and Udon Noodles. IUdon and soba are typical noodle dishes in Japan.
